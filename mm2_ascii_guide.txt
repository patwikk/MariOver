MM2 ASCII Character Guide (Collision-Free Extended ASCII Standard)
==================================================================
Convention: UPPERCASE / Extended Characters = solid / dangerous / unique items
            lowercase / Standard Characters = passable / collectible / basic terrain
            [Space] = empty air
            #       = ground tile
